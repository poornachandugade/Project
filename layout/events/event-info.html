NMF volkswagen vento cup

Volkswagen Motorsport India have been around for around six years now and have been a stepping stone for young and enthusiastic drivers to showcase their skills and talent. Volkswagen now join hands with Madras Motor Sport Club (MMSC) for the next three seasons starting from 2016. The Volkswagen Vento Cup 2016 will now be a part of the FMSCI Indian National Racing Championship. With this new association, MRF will be the new tyre partners for the 2016 Vento Cup.
